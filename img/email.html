I wanted to introduce to you the game, Bomber Brigade. It is a 3D action and a strategy-based platform game that involves the player tackling enemies while trying to save the platform from getting destroyed by bombs. The platform is divided into tiles. The bombs drop on the tiles and wait for a few seconds before exploding and taking away the tile. The player must push away the bomb from the platform before it explodes all this while trying to strategically destroy or avoid other kinds of enemies that harm the player. The main catch here is that each tile is linked to human life and the player must try his best to save as many lives as he can. From time to time, humans provide him with some special temporary powers in the form of pickup items.

The story goes as follows: The user plays as a Bomb Disposal Officer and tries to save multiple planets across the Universe. A group of evil aliens plans to destroy all planets one by one so that they can take over. The user steps into the shoes of Jack, the Bomb disposal officer, tries to foil their evil plans by climbing up multiple levels to eventually fight the aliens in a final fight.
